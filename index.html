Welcome to PCG AWOL Division

We are responsible for handling AWOL cases and maintaining personnel accountability in the Philippine Coast Guard.

About Us

The PCG AWOL Division is tasked with monitoring, investigating, and resolving cases of personnel absent without official leave. Our mission is to uphold accountability, maintain discipline, and ensure operational readiness within the Philippine Coast Guard.

Our Services

  • AWOL case investigation and resolution
  • Personnel accountability monitoring
  • Coordination with relevant PCG units
  • Policy enforcement and advisory
